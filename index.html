Famous Formula One Drivers

Ayrton Senna

A Brazilian icon known for his fearless driving and 3 World Championships. Senna remains a legend in F1 history, admired for his raw talent and passion.

Lewis Hamilton

A 7-time World Champion from the UK, Hamilton is known for his smooth driving style, dominance in the 2010s, and being one of the sport's most influential figures.

Max Verstappen

The current face of F1, Verstappen drives for Red Bull Racing and is known for his bold overtakes, consistency, and back-to-back championship wins.
